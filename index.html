Dr. Sachin Kumar Yadav is a dedicated orthopedic surgeon specializing in sports medicine and arthroscopic surgeries. With extensive training and experience in the field, he is committed to providing the highest standard of care to his patients.

After completing his MBBS and MS in Orthopedic Surgery, Dr. Yadav has focused on minimally invasive techniques to treat sports injuries, joint disorders, and degenerative conditions. His approach emphasizes accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and comprehensive rehabilitation.

Dr. Yadav stays at the forefront of orthopedic advancements through continuous education and research. He has contributed to several publications and regularly attends international conferences to exchange knowledge with peers worldwide.

Understanding Knee Osteoarthritis

Knee osteoarthritis is a common degenerative joint disease affecting millions worldwide. As we age, the protective cartilage in our joints wears down, leading to pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility.

Key Prevention Strategies:

  • Maintain healthy weight to reduce joint stress
  • Engage in low-impact exercises like swimming
  • Strengthen quadriceps muscles for better support
  • Consider glucosamine supplements after consultation

Sports Injury Prevention for Athletes

Whether you're a weekend warrior or competitive athlete, preventing sports injuries should be a top priority. Many common injuries can be avoided with proper preparation and technique.
